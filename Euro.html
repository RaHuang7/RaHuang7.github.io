Course Overview

Welcome to European History, the history option you're given if you opt for Humanities. I'll be honest, this course isn't exactly the easiest and throughout the school year you can expect to face many tests, seminars and art assignments. The school year will culminate with a final art project as well as the dreaded AP European exam so you can expect this to be your new best friend. But don't worry, as long as you study and try your best you'll make it! So without further ado let's go over what you can expect to face in the year!
